Shaping the world…
He Gave His Life · a journey
In the beginning

God made the world good.

There was no distance between God and man, because there was no sin in the world. And God walked with people.

Genesis 1:27 · Psalm 139:14
But something changed

"I'm a good person."

But the Bible says your sins "have made a separation between you and your God."

Isaiah 59:2
The problem

Mankind chose sin.

We are all sinners — born "dead in our sins, separated from Christ, having no hope and without God in the world."

Ephesians 2:1, 12

God is not a distant idea who shrugs at wrong. He is the holy judge of the earth, and he must punish evil.

We can't fix it ourselves

We try — saying prayers, being nice, attending church. But nothing we do can bridge this gap. "For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God."

Romans 3:23
But God

He demonstrates his love.

"While we were still sinners, Christ died for us."

Romans 5:8
The good news

He gave his life.

Jesus died on the cross to take the punishment we deserved. Three days later he rose from the dead — proof that God accepted his sacrifice. Anyone who trusts in him "shall be saved."

Romans 10:9
A free gift

"By grace you have been saved through faith. It is the gift of God." Repentance is turning from your sin. Faith is trusting in Jesus's sacrifice alone for the forgiveness of your sins.

Ephesians 2:8

Will you receive
this free gift?

Will you trust in Christ alone for the forgiveness of your sins?

A new creation

When God forgives you, he makes you new and his Spirit lives in you. One day Jesus will return and make a new heavens and a new earth, and all who trust in him will walk with God forever.

2 Corinthians 5:17 · Revelation 21:3–4
